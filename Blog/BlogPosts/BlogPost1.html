Building a rocking plane

this process was pretty interesting. my cousin and I spent a couple days to build this we used maple and walnut to create the plane. we cut out the general shapes on a bandsaw for the rockers. the propeller moves on its own. I turned a nose cone on the lathe and we cut out the propeller blade. this allows the propeller to spin.
